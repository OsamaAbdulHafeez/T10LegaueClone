T10 Cricket or Ten10 Cricket is a shorter form of cricket that is played annually every year. Two teams play a single inning, bounded to ten overs per team for approximately 90 minutes. This year, the T10 Cricket League, Zim Afro T10, is being held in Zimbabwe, starting on 20 July, 2023.
